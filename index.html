Dutch's 3D Printer

This is my 3D printer controller. I can access it anywhere I have internet and control my 3D printer using this website.

The camera feed you see below is livestreamed on an unlisted Youtube livestream, and the camera is attatched to a custom robotic arm that is only controllable if you login to the control panel below the livestream. I think it's pretty cool stuff. I also have another robotic arm rigged onto the touchscreen of the printer. This arm allows me to stop or start prints, pre-heat the bed and nozzle, and rehome the nozzle.

As of right now, the website looks pretty ugly but I plan on making it look better just for fun. Right now, my main goal is to get the site set up and then polish everything afterward. I'm also going to make any services I provide easy to use. Any feedback would be greatly appreciated.
